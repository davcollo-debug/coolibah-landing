👋 About Beth

I'm Beth — a qualified Social Worker and independent support worker operating under Qualified Wellness Supports, based in Ipswich and servicing the greater Brisbane area.

I've been working in the forensic mental health sector since 2017 and providing support work since 2015 with adults, children and young people aged 2–17. I practice from a strengths-based, trauma-informed and person-centred framework, bringing both professional expertise and lived experience to connect authentically and respectfully with the people I support.

I have extensive experience working with individuals living with major mental health disorders, as well as young people involved in the child safety system. As a mum of two young children, I bring warmth, patience and a nurturing approach to my work. I'm known for being reliable, flexible and creative in how I support people to achieve their goals and improve their quality of life.

I'm passionate about walking alongside people as they build capacity, confidence and meaningful change.

I have lived experience of mental health conditions, which gives me a deeper understanding and empathy for the people I support. I'm also proudly LGBTQ+ friendly and committed to creating a safe, inclusive space for everyone.
